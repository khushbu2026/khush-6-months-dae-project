**GitHub Pages Portfolio Checklist**

This checklist will help you build a **professional portfolio** that meets the program’s standards.

Tick off each item as you complete it.

**Career Aspirations (About Me Section) / Landing Page**

☐ Share your **future goals**: the type of work environment you’re looking for, preferred roles, ideal job description, and what kind of team you want to work with.

☐ Include a personal **“About Me”** section that highlights your interests, values, and aspirations.

**GitHub Pages Organization**

☐ Organize your GitHub Pages into **clear, well-structured sections** for better readability and navigation.

☐ **Clean your GitHub repositories** so they are less cluttered (delete unused files, add README.md, clear old branches).

**Branding**

☐ Add the **official dae logo** to your homepage and any additional GitHub Pages.

**Personal Details**

☐ Display your **full name** on your landing page.

☐ Display your **graduation date** on your landing page.

**Profile Photo**

☐ Upload a **clear, professional headshot** to personalize your profile.

**Extra for a Strong Portfolio (Recommended)**

☐ Link to at least **2–3 of your best projects** with descriptions and images.

☐ Make sure your portfolio looks **good on both mobile and desktop**.

☐ Add **contact information** (email, LinkedIn, GitHub link).

☐ Keep **consistent fonts, colors, and spacing**.
